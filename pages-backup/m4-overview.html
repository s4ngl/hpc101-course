Lesson 1: Data Transfer Methods

  

Choose a Transfer Tool

Pick between SCP, SFTP, Globus, and HSI/HTAR for each situation, including PHI and on-campus constraints.

  

Move and Archive Files

Lesson 2: Resource Management Best Practices

  

Right-Size Your Requests

Measure what a job actually uses and tighten CPU, memory, and wall time to keep the queue fair.

  

Manage Jobs and Get Help

Read pending-job reasons, control running jobs, keep storage tidy, and know where to find support.
