About This Course

Want to learn about Generative Artificial Intelligence (GenAI)? This course will introduce you to the principles of GenAI and get you started using tools available here at IU. 

It should take you around three and a half hours to watch all of the video content. There are also 8 reflection exercises and most will take less than 20 minutes to complete.

Disclaimer

By joining this course, you agree to these ground rules:

  Rule #1: AI Isn’t Perfect

Treat GenAI like someone you just met: don't automatically assume it has all the answers.

  What Rule #1 Means

GenAI tools can be wrong, biased, or make up facts entirely — even quotes and citations. Don’t take outputs at face value. Your job? Verify everything before using or sharing it.

  Rule #2: Play Fair & Stay Legal

Honesty matters. Don't use GenAI when you shouldn't, and disclose when you do.

  What Rule #2 Means

Stick to the rules: course policies, university codes, professional standards, and the law. If you used AI, say so—transparency is part of integrity.

  Rule #3: Protect Private Stuff

Don’t share anything you wouldn't want posted on the Internet. Your information may be shared with others or used to train the AI.

  What Rule #3 Means

Don’t feed the AI any private, sensitive, or restricted data. That includes things covered by FERPA, HIPAA, or IU’s privacy policies.

  Rule #4: No Guarantees

You're in charge of your AI use, but you're also responsible for it — even if you're using it through IU.

  What Rule #4 Means 

We (your instructors and IU) don’t guarantee AI results. If something goes sideways, we’re not responsible for damages or confusion caused by AI tools.
